Input: nums: a list of items
Output: result: a tuple containing the item with the maximum frequency and its frequency

1: Initialize a dictionary `dict` to store the frequency of each item in `nums`
2: for each item `i` in `nums`:
3:     Increment the count of `i` in `dict` by 1
4: Find the item with the maximum frequency in `dict` and store it in `result`
5: return `result`
