[BEGIN]
Input: n: an integer representing the number of first natural numbers to consider
Output: average: the average of the cubes of the first n natural numbers

1: Initialize a variable sum to 0 to store the sum of cubes
2: For each integer i from 1 to n (inclusive):
3:     Calculate the cube of i (i * i * i)
4:     Add the cube of i to sum
5: Calculate the average by dividing sum by n
6: Round the average to 6 decimal places
7: Return the rounded average
[END]
