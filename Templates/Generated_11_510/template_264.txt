Input: h_age: human age (integer)
Output: d_age: dog age (integer)

1: if h_age is less than 0:
2:     exit the function
3: if h_age is less than or equal to 2:
4:     calculate d_age as h_age multiplied by 10.5
5: else:
6:     calculate d_age as 21 plus (h_age minus 2) multiplied by 4
7: return d_age
