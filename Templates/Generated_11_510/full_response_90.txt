[BEGIN]
Input: list1: a list of strings  
Output: max_length: the length of the longest word in list1  
1: Set max_length to the length of the first word in list1  
2: for each word i in list1:  
3:     if the length of i is greater than max_length:  
4:         Set max_length to the length of i  
5: return max_length  
[END]
