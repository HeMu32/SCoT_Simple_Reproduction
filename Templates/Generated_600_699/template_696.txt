Input: list1, list2: two lists of lists
Output: result: a new list of lists where each element is the concatenation of the corresponding elements from list1 and list2

1: Initialize an empty list called result
2: For each pair of sublists (sublist1, sublist2) from list1 and list2:
3:     Concatenate sublist1 and sublist2
4:     Append the concatenated sublist to result
5: Return result
