Input: li: a list of numbers, min: a number, max: a number
Output: count: the number of elements in li that are within the range [min, max]
1: Initialize a counter variable ctr to 0
2: Iterate over each element x in the list li
3:     If x is greater than or equal to min and less than or equal to max:
4:         Increment ctr by 1
5: Return ctr
