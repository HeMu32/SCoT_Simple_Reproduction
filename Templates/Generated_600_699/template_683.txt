Input: n: an integer  
Output: True or False  
1: Initialize i to 1  
2: While i squared is less than or equal to n:  
3:     Initialize j to 1  
4:     While j squared is less than or equal to n:  
5:         If i squared plus j squared equals n:  
6:             Return True  
7:         Increment j by 1  
8:     Increment i by 1  
9: Return False
